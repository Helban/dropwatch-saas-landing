FAQ

Questions, answered

Which stores does Dropwatch support?

Any store with a public product page. There is no per-store setup: you paste the link and Dropwatch reads the live price and stock status from the page itself.

How fast are the alerts?

Free watches are checked daily. Pro re-checks every 5 minutes and Business every minute, so flash sales and short restocks still reach you in time.

Do I need a browser extension?

No. Dropwatch runs in the cloud. There is nothing to install and nothing running on your machine.

Where do alerts arrive?

Email on every plan, plus Telegram and Slack from Pro upward. You can send a single watch to more than one channel.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Plans are month to month, and downgrading back to Free keeps your existing watches up to the Free limit.
